Add your three Figma frames as full-screen splash backgrounds:

  public/splash/home-1.png   (artboard Home-1)
  public/splash/home-2.png   (artboard Home-2)
  public/splash/home-3.png   (artboard Home-3)

Export each frame from Rosi.fig as PNG @2x (1080×1920 or your frame size).

The app cross-fades them in a loop (Home-1 → Home-2 → Home-3 → repeat).
The glass Start button sits on top; your frame artwork shows through the blur.
